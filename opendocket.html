Why Open Docket exists

Not everyone can afford a lawyer to find the right form. Not everyone knows what forms exist. Open Docket was built for them — a completely free, open source legal form finder that speaks plain English. No subscriptions. No signups. No gatekeeping. Just people with problems and the forms to help solve them.
